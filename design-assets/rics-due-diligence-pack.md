## 2. AI Systems Summary

### 2.1 What "AI-Assisted" Means

Our site intelligence reports are produced using a multi-agent artificial intelligence system. "AI-assisted" means:

- AI agents are used for **data acquisition** (querying authoritative public data sources)
- AI agents are used for **analysis and scoring** (interpreting data into risk assessments)
- AI agents are used for **initial report drafting** (assembling findings into structured documents)
- All outputs pass through a **multi-stage quality assurance pipeline**
- All outputs are **reviewed by our engineering team** before delivery

AI does not make autonomous decisions about report conclusions or recommendations.

## 5. Quality Assurance Pipeline

Every report passes through a 24-category QA pipeline before delivery. The pipeline covers five quality domains:

### 5.1 Accuracy
- Factual accuracy verification against source data
- Numerical precision checking
- Citation validity confirmation

### 5.2 Regulatory Compliance
- Planning policy cross-referencing (NPPF, local plan, SPDs)
- Building regulations compliance (where applicable)
- Environmental regulatory alignment

### 5.3 Consistency
- Cross-report data agreement
- Terminology consistency
- Internal logic validation

### 5.4 Formatting
- Document structure verification
- Visual asset validation (maps, charts, tables)
- Table integrity checking

### 5.5 Citation Integrity
- Source attribution verification
- Data freshness confirmation
- Reference link validity

Individual QA check names, pass/fail criteria, and scoring algorithms are proprietary.

## 6. Human Oversight Model

### 6.1 Engineering Review
Every report is reviewed by a member of our engineering team before delivery. The reviewer checks the report for accuracy, regulatory relevance, and professional appropriateness.

### 6.2 Accountability
The engineering reviewer is accountable for the final report content. AI systems do not make autonomous decisions about conclusions, recommendations, or risk ratings.

### 6.3 No Autonomous Delivery
No AI-generated report is delivered to a client without human review. There is no automated delivery pathway that bypasses human oversight.

### 6.4 Feedback Loop
Quality issues identified in delivered reports are fed back into the QA pipeline as new checks, ensuring continuous improvement.

## 7. Data Governance

### 7.1 Client Data Processing
- Client data (site addresses, project details) is processed **solely** for the purpose of producing the ordered report
- Client data is **not** used to train, fine-tune, or improve any AI models
- Data is processed on secure infrastructure

### 7.2 Data Retention
- Contact form data: retained for up to 12 months
- Order data: retained for 6 years (UK tax/accounting requirements)
- Report processing data: retained only for the duration of report production

### 7.3 Regulatory Compliance
- All data processing complies with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018
- See our published Privacy Policy for full details: pfandco.co.uk/privacy-policy

### 7.4 Third-Party AI Services
Where we use third-party AI models or services, we assess them against data processing policies, security standards, and alignment with our governance framework.
