Harry's Briefing Pack — What This Is & How To Use It

What Is Site Intelligence?

Site Intelligence is a planning research automation platform. When a developer or planning consultancy needs to assess a site — checking constraints, flood risk, heritage, ecology, transport, ground conditions, policy compliance, viability — their planners currently spend days or weeks doing desk-based research across dozens of data sources, then writing it up into reports.

We do all of that in 48 hours. 125 AI agents pull data from 23 official sources (Environment Agency, BGS, Historic England, MAGIC, OS, Land Registry, etc.), cross-reference it, and produce submission-ready reports through a 4-gate quality pipeline. 74 report templates covering everything from flood risk assessments to viability appraisals to heritage impact assessments.

The One-Liner

"We do the desk work so your planners can do the planning."

Why These Companies?

Every company in this pack is actively hiring planners right now. The UK has an 884-vacancy planning staff shortage — only 21% of planning departments are fully staffed. These firms are spending £35,000-70,000+ per hire, waiting 4+ months to fill roles, and losing senior staff time to junior-level desk research.

Our pitch: Instead of hiring another planner to do data gathering, use Site Intelligence. Same output, 48 hours, fraction of the cost. Your existing planners then spend 100% of their time on the human stuff — site visits, client meetings, professional judgment, advocacy — the work that actually earns fees.

The Economics (Harry's Killer Stat)

A mid-level planner costs £42,000-52,000/year fully loaded (salary + NI + pension + workspace + RTPI). They take 4+ months to hire and 6-12 months to train. They handle ~150 cases/year and spend 60-75% of their time on desk research that SI automates.

SI's Feasibility product at £1,295/project = £19,425 for 15 projects. That's the desk research output of roughly one planner, at half the cost, with no recruitment risk, no training time, and 48-hour turnaround.

What SI Does NOT Do (Important for Credibility)

We're very clear about this — it builds trust:

  • No site visits — all desktop. Their planners still do walkovers and surveys.
  • No professional sign-off — their chartered planners review and sign off. Their PI insurance, their brand.
  • No client meetings — relationship management stays with them.
  • No planning committee / appeal advocacy — we give them the intelligence and case law; they make the arguments.

Position it as: "We're the engine, you're the driver. Your brand, your client relationships, your professional judgment. We just make the research part instant."
