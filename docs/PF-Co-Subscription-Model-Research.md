## 6. Proposed Subscription Tiers

### Operating Model: Manual Verification
- No self-serve interface currently — clients submit sites, PF & Co runs the system and verifies outputs

| **Target** | Architects, small developers | Active developers, planning consultants | Volume housebuilders, land promoters |

### Value Anchoring

- **Scout at £199/month** sits between PlanningBot (£49) and LandInsight Pro (£180) — but delivers actual verified reports
- **Professional at £499/month** undercuts a single traditional consultant report (£1,000–2,500) while giving 4 screenings + a full pack
- **Enterprise at £999/month** is a fraction of Glenigan (£8K–50K/year) and includes actual deliverables
- A Professional subscriber gets roughly £10K–£15K worth of traditional consultant work per month

## 8. Capacity Planning

### Time Per Deliverable (Estimated)
| Deliverable | System Time | Human QA/Verification | Total |
|-------------|-----------|----------------------|-------|
| Site screening | ~1 hour (automated) | 2–3 hours | 3–4 hours |
| Full report pack | ~2–4 hours (automated) | 8–12 hours | 10–16 hours |

### Revenue Ceiling by Team Size
| Team Size | Screenings/month | Full Packs/month | Revenue Ceiling |
|-----------|-----------------|------------------|-----------------|
| 1 person (you) | ~20 | ~8 | ~£10K/month |
| You + 1 hire | ~40 | ~16 | ~£20K/month |
| Team of 3 | ~60 | ~24 | ~£35K/month |

### Key Insight
The bottleneck is human verification, not system capacity. As QA experience builds:
- Verification time will reduce (3 hours → 1.5 hours per screening)
- Pattern recognition improves — common site types become faster
- QA checklists can be templated for junior reviewers

## 10. Recommendations

1. **Launch with Scout + Professional only** — don't offer Enterprise until delivery model is proven
2. **Cap total subscribers initially** (e.g., 15) — scarcity creates urgency and protects quality
3. **Track hours per project type from day one** — this data tells you when to hire and what to reprice
4. **The upsell is where the real money is** — subscriptions get clients in, discounted full packs drive revenue
5. **Consider a "Founding Member" discount** for the first 10 subscribers (e.g., 20% off for life) — builds loyalty and case studies
6. **Review pricing quarterly** — as verification gets faster, margins improve and tier limits can increase
