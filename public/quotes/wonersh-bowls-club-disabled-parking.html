Site & Project

Construction of Disabled Parking Area
Wonersh Bowls Club, Wonersh Common, Surrey
Area: 40 m²  |  Duration: approx. 1 week
Site electrics, water & welfare provided by client

Scope of Works

Formation of a new disabled-accessible parking area (40 m²) comprising excavation to formation level, MOT Type 1 sub-base installation (150 mm compacted depth), heavy-duty weed prevention membrane, commercial-grade gravel stabilisation grid system suitable for vehicular loading and DDA-compliant wheelchair access, gravel infill, and 18 linear metres of granite set kerb edging. Works include careful excavation around existing underground services (soil pipes, electrical cables and water supply pipes) and full disposal of excavated material.

Assumptions & Advisory Notes

Type 1 Quantity: 10 tonnes of Type 1 at a compacted density of approx. 1.9 t/m³ will yield approximately 5.3 m³ loose, compacting to around 4.2 m³ or roughly 105 mm depth over 40 m². To achieve the full 150 mm compacted depth, approximately 14 tonnes may be required. We have priced a single 10-tonne load as instructed; an additional delivery can be arranged at cost if required on site.

Underground Services: We have allowed for careful hand-digging around known services. Should any unforeseen services, obstructions or ground conditions (e.g. rock, contaminated soil, high water table) be encountered, additional costs may apply and will be agreed before proceeding.

Access: Quotation assumes adequate vehicle access for a grab lorry and aggregate delivery vehicle to reach the works area.
