What We Deliver

Six capabilities in one platform

⚙
23-Point Automated Constraint Screening
Every site is scanned against flood zones, listed buildings, conservation areas, ecology designations, green belt, AONB, air quality, contamination, archaeology, and 14 more constraint datasets — all from live government APIs. Traffic-light ratings tell you instantly what you're dealing with.

⚠
CIL & S106 Forecasting

Who It's For

We do the heavy data lifting.
You make the decisions.

We're not here to replace anyone. We're here to do the data crunching that currently takes weeks and costs thousands — so developers, consultants, and architects can move faster and make better-informed decisions. England-wide coverage.

🤝
How we work with you
We handle the data-intensive groundwork — pulling live government data, cross-referencing constraints, generating the technical reports. You keep doing what you do best: making strategic decisions, managing client relationships, and applying your professional expertise. We make the whole process faster for everyone.
Land Promoters
Screening sites for option agreements
You're assessing multiple sites and need to know which ones are worth promoting before committing to option fees. We do the data crunching — constraint analysis, planning risk scores, development economics — in hours rather than weeks. You screen at volume and focus your time on the sites with genuine potential.
Key reports
  • Site Feasibility Report
  • Market Intelligence
  • CIL & S106 Assessment
  • Development Strategy
Developers
Residential schemes of all sizes across England
Every pound spent pre-planning is at risk until you get consent. We front-load the data crunching — flood, heritage, ecology, transport, CIL, market analysis — so you know exactly what you're dealing with before committing serious spend. We tell you which specialist surveys are actually needed, so you stop paying for things just in case.
Key reports
  • Full Submission Pack
  • Concept Feasibility Study
  • Pre-Application Pack
  • Flood Risk Assessment
Planning Consultants
Your data engine, not your replacement
You bring the planning strategy and client relationships. We handle the heavy technical data work — pulling the constraint data, generating the supporting documents, cross-referencing against policy and precedent. Flood risk, heritage, transport, D&A — same-day turnaround so you can move faster for your clients.
Key reports
  • Heritage Statement
  • Transport Statement
  • Design & Access Statement
  • BNG Screening
Architects & Designers
Constraint-informed design from day one
Before you start designing, you need to know what the site allows. We crunch the constraint data and give you the design parameters up front — heritage sensitivities, flood zones, height restrictions, density expectations — so your scheme responds to real constraints from day one, not surprises at validation.
